# Dividend Lab

> Building the best platform for long-term dividend investors.

This document answers: **What is Dividend Lab?**

For brand personality and desired feeling, see `design/BRAND.md`. For visual rules, see `design/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`. For engineering expectations, see `standards/ENGINEERING_STANDARD.md`.

---

## Vision

Dividend Lab exists to become the most trusted platform for long-term dividend investors.

The platform should help investors build wealth through education, data and discipline rather than speculation or short-term trading.

Dividend Lab is not a brokerage. It is an analysis platform, education platform and community built for investors who think in years instead of days.

---

## Mission

Help people make better long-term investment decisions through:

- high-quality data
- portfolio analysis
- dividend tracking
- education
- community
- artificial intelligence

The goal is to help users become better investors, not tell them what to buy.

---

## Core Principles

Dividend Lab always prioritizes:

1. Long-term investing
2. Data over opinions
3. Education over predictions
4. Quality over quantity
5. Simplicity over complexity

Every product decision should support these principles.

---

## Product Scope

Dividend Lab is:

- a portfolio analysis platform
- a dividend tracking platform
- an education platform
- an AI-assisted investing companion
- a long-term planning workspace
- a high-signal investing community
- a premium SaaS product for dividend investors

Dividend Lab is not:

- a broker
- a trading platform
- a day-trading tool
- a stock-picking service
- a financial advisor
- a market hype product

See `design/BRAND.md` for the full product boundary.

---

## Target User

Dividend Lab is built for investors who:

- invest monthly
- build passive income
- enjoy portfolio optimization
- like statistics
- think in decades

The product is not designed for day traders or short-term speculators.

## Long-Term Goal

Dividend Lab should become the platform that serious dividend investors open every day before looking at their broker.

It should become synonymous with long-term dividend investing.
